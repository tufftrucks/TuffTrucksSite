ABOUT

TUFF TRUCKS is a refreshing twist on NFT/Blockchain gaming. The team put together a playable ALPHA build to generate a smaller, but stronger community of players and holders so we can consistently get feedback as we build out the multiplayer and full release version of the game, alongside tokenomics, smart contract design and more. We feel that the community NEEDS to be first and we're very excited to be building out our team from within our community. If you've been wanting to get involved with an NFT project rather than just holding, this is DEFINITELY a great place for that<3

FAQ

What's the supply?

We are estimating a maximum of 400 ALPHA COLLECTION TUFF TRUCK NFTs to be released, with NON-ALPHA collections being released later. ALPHA COLLECTION NFTs will recieve the highest level of perks including but not limited to airdrops of NFTs, ERC20s, $ETH, $MATIC and more...

When do we launch?

We have not announced any estimated date of release for the ALPHA COLLECTION.NOTE: We will never have a stealth-launch, please be careful of scams. Official launch details will be shared on our website, Discord, and our Twitter page.

What's the utility?

Holders of ALPHA TUFF TRUCKS will first and foremost have a useable truck in-game, access to our Early Supporter community where feedback is king for our community developed-game, holder-exclusive contests and airdrops, priority in future collections, and other events!

Where's the Roadmap?

The TUFF TRUCKS ALPHA Launch means more than just getting in early; it means your opinion, feedback and playtime will be both valued & rewarded. We're keeping the ALPA MINT small so that holders will have the ability to be play-testers, designers, influencers, devs artists and more for the project. We have plenty of ideas like in-game boost being a burnable ERC20, $BOOST, to collaborations with popular NFT artrists on future truck collections to player designed tracks where you can earn a commission on every race ran through it. We're going to build this project together and we need your help<3

How do I buy?

We will likely be listing on Ethereum or Polygon but we're open to suggestions prior to the ALPHA release (a great example of early community membership allowing you to have a voice) but there's no estimated date just yet. Please join the Discord for updates!
